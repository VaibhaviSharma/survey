A pay-per-use service allows users to watch a single movie, TV show, or sports game on digital platforms without needing a full subscription. It provides flexible, on-demand access by letting users pay only for the specific content they want, rather than committing to monthly or yearly plans. This model caters to occasional viewers or those seeking exclusive events, offering a cost-effective alternative for tailored entertainment consumption.
